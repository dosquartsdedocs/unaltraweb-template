A manual normally needs more than a list of topics. It needs a stable weekly rhythm, clear preparation tasks and visible practical outcomes.

## Weekly rhythm

Each week can combine a short conceptual reading, a lab activity and a reflection prompt.

### Before class

Students read the chapter summary and check the vocabulary.

### During class

The teacher can use diagrams, datasets and callouts to keep the practical session grounded.

## Keywords

Search should find words across the book, including coordinates, raster, vector, fieldwork and assessment.
