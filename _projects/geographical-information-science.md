Personal academic sites often need something between a blog post and a publication list. A project card can represent a research line, a method family, a teaching sequence, or a software ecosystem.

## Research Line Structure

| Theme | Example material | Page component to test |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Spatial theory | Notes on place, distance, scale, and uncertainty. | Explanatory text |
| Methods | Classification, overlay, network analysis, simulation. | Tables and diagrams |
| Teaching | Course units, slides, notebooks, assignments. | Lists and links |
| Software | Packages, scripts, reproducible workflows. | Repository links |
