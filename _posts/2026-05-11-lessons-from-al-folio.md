al-folio is a useful starting point for publication-heavy academic websites. It has bibliography support, project cards, posts, and a mature visual language.

For `unaltraweb`, the goal is to keep the useful ideas while removing assumptions that do not fit: external build-time scraping, inherited demo content, and a single-site mindset.

The platform should be static by default and extensible by site profile.
